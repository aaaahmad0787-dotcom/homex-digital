What We Do

Our Services

From digital door locks to wardrobe security — Homex Digital Services covers all your smart lock needs with professional installation and after-sales support.

Digital Door Lock Installation

Professional fitting of fingerprint, PIN, RFID and app-controlled digital locks on main doors, bedrooms, and office cabins.

Mechanical Door Lock

Premium mechanical locks for main doors and security doors. High-durability mortise and rim locks with anti-pick protection.

Office Lock Installation

Smart access control and digital locks for offices, cabins, server rooms and restricted areas with multi-user management.

Wardrobe & Furniture Lock

Fingerprint and PIN locks for wardrobes, cabinets, drawers and furniture. Clean installation with zero damage to finish.

Commercial & Hotel Lock

Card-based and master-key system locks for hotels, guesthouses and commercial complexes. Bulk supply and installation available.

Repair, AMC & Support

Lock repair, battery replacement, PIN reset and Annual Maintenance Contracts. 24/7 support for all lock brands and models.
